We were able to begin tracking traffic to our app using Google Analytics and also began running A/B testing using Optimizely. After 2 days of accepting traffic, the app has tracked 4,100 unique visitors and tested their engagement by adding "RSVP" in front of event listings on the homepage. Adding "RSVP" increased clickthrough rates from 5% to 8%. Further results from this first A/B test can be found here.
