From Us to You.


We are four friends and classmates who endeavor to make your student life easier. As first-year MBAs, we were overwhelmed by the huge number of academic, career, and social events to choose from. There was no intuitive, fun way to manage our events, so we created XCG. Now, rather than spending hours searching for and scheduling our events, we can focus on what matters in business school:
Work Hard, Play Harder!
